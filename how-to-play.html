HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE
Face 3 opponents in a circular arena. Deplete their stamina through collisions to "burst" them. Be the last blade spinning to win.
LAUNCHING
Before battle, you must launch your blade into the arena. The direction and power of your launch determines your opening position.
COMBAT CONTROLS
Two actions available once your blade is active. Mastering the timing between them is the key to winning.
STAMINA & BURST
Every blade has a stamina bar. Collisions drain it. Drifting near the arena edge drains it faster. Hit zero — you burst.

STATS EXPLAINED
Five stats, each with a distinct in-game effect. Understanding them changes everything about how you pick and play your blade.
ATK
ATTACK POWER
How hard your hits drain the enemy's stamina. Every collision deals base force × your ATK × their DEF. High ATK turns even glancing blows into brutal stamina drains.
BIG BANG PEGASIS ATK 15 — highest in the game DRACIEL SHELL ATK 1 — barely tickles opponents
DEF
DAMAGE REDUCTION
Reduces how much stamina you lose per collision. High DEF means each hit hurts less. Important: DEF does NOT protect against bursting — that's BST's job. A blade can have DEF 13 and still burst if its BST is low.
PHANTOM ORION DEF 13 — barely loses stamina from any hit BIG BANG PEGASIS DEF 5 — bleeds fast when caught
STA
STAMINA POOL
Your total health. As it drains, your burst vulnerability rises sharply — a blade at 30% stamina can be one-shot burst by the same hit that would only sting at full health. Larger pools give you more room to survive bad exchanges and edge drain.
PHANTOM ORION STA 15 — 230 points, takes forever to empty SHARK EDGE STA 2 — 68 points, drains in seconds
SPD
SPEED & IMPACT FORCE
Faster, lighter blades generate more impact force on collision — which amplifies both stamina damage and burst pressure dealt. A fast blade slamming at full speed deals significantly more burst damage than a slow heavy blade nudging the same opponent. Heavier blades are harder to push around but hit softer.
VIPER TAIL SPD 10 — hits like a bullet ROCK BISON SPD 1 — slow, immovable wall
BST
BURST RESISTANCE — THE HIDDEN DANGER ⚠
Controls whether a collision instantly bursts you — independent of your stamina bar. Burst damage is calculated separately from stamina damage: impact force × enemy ATK × (1 − your BST) × worn factor. The worn factor scales from 0.15 at full health to 1.0 at zero stamina — meaning as you take hits, you become exponentially more vulnerable to a single big collision finishing you instantly. A BST 1 blade can be one-shot by any high-ATK opponent once its stamina dips below ~60%.
PHANTOM ORION BST 15 — cannot be burst under any circumstances KNIGHT SHIELD BST 10 — near-impossible to one-shot SHARK EDGE BST 1 — one good hit when worn = instant burst DRAN SWORD BST 2 — glass cannon, dies how it lives
CAUTION: THE BST TRAP
Some blades have BST so high they are nearly impossible to burst — but their ATK can still devastate you. If your BST is low, colliding with one of these can instantly burst you while they barely notice. Always check your opponent's BST before playing aggressively.
BURST DAMAGE YOU TAKE = impact force × ENEMY ATK × (1 − YOUR BST) × worn factor
Worn factor rises from 0.15 at full health to 1.0 at zero stamina — every hit you take makes the next one more dangerous.
A blade with BST 15 takes near-zero burst damage from any hit. A blade with BST 1 can be finished by a single hard collision once worn down.
TIER SYSTEM
Higher tiers have meaningfully stronger stats. Skilled play can upset one tier below. Beating two tiers down requires a miracle.
MYTHICAL
Near-peak power. Dominant in their domain with extraordinary specials.
LEGENDARY
Elite warriors with game-changing abilities. Top-tier threats.
CHAMPION
Strong all-rounders. Competitive in any arena with the right play.
ELITE
Powerful specialists. Excellent when matched to the right arena.
ADVANCED
Skilled contenders. Viable with good special timing and arena choice.
APPRENTICE
Learning fighters. Need a skill edge or favorable arena to compete.
NOVICE
Starter blades. Good for learning — outmatched against higher tiers.
SPECIAL MOVES
Hold to charge over 2 seconds, then release for a unique ability. A well-timed special can flip a losing fight. 8-second cooldown after use.

TIPS & STRATEGY
⏳
Save your special for the right moment. Don't use it at full health — wait until you're cornered, outnumbered, or an enemy is one good hit from bursting.
🌋
Use the edge as a weapon. Tap near the wall to knock opponents into the edge-drain zone. Near the rim, stamina evaporates — one push can decide a match.
🏟
Match your type to the arena. ATTACK thrives in Volcano Crater, STAMINA/DEFENSE dominate Shadow Void and Frozen Tundra.
👁
Let enemies tire each other out. You start 1v3 — hang back after launching and boost in only once someone is already weakened. Gang-up damage stacks fast.
⚖
Tier gap is real. Fighting two tiers above you is nearly impossible. Focus the lowest-tier enemy first, use specials aggressively, and pick an arena that favors your type.
OBJECTIVE
Boost your Beyblade upward through 10 increasingly dangerous stages. Collect all stars and reach the glowing finish platform at the top.
CONTROLS
Single tap/click controls — works on both desktop and touch screens.
TAP / CLICK
Boost toward the tapped location. Your blade launches in that direction with fixed force.
COOLDOWN
Each boost has a 3.5-second cooldown. The bar refills — don't waste boosts or you'll fall.
GRAVITY
Your blade falls constantly. Land on platforms to stop falling and line up your next boost.
WALLS
Side walls bounce you back. Wall-bouncing is useful for reaching platforms that seem too far.
PLATFORM TYPES
Five platform types, each with different behavior. Stages introduce more complex types as difficulty increases.
NORMAL
Solid and permanent. Safe to land on as many times as you need.
FRAGILE
Cracks and disappears after you land. One use only!
MOVING
Slides left and right continuously. Time your boost to land on it.
BOOST
Launches you upward on contact — a free shortcut higher.
FINISH
Glows green at the very top. Reach it to complete the stage.
STAGE PROGRESSION
10 stages from easy intro to expert gauntlet. The bar shows relative difficulty.
TIPS & STRATEGY
⏱
Guard your boost cooldown. 3.5 seconds is a long time while falling. Never burn a boost unless you're sure you won't need another one immediately after.
🟡
Prioritize boost platforms. Yellow boost pads send you flying upward for free — route your climb to hit them instead of grinding normal platforms.
⚠
Fragile platforms vanish on landing. Plan your route before you touch down — if the next boost requires landing there again, you'll fall through nothing.
↔
Use moving platform momentum. A moving platform at its edge acts like a slingshot sideways. Use this to reach platforms that look impossible from a standing position.
★
Plan your star route. Stars cluster near platforms. Scout the layout from below and pick a path that sweeps stars naturally — don't chase them mid-air and waste your boost.
